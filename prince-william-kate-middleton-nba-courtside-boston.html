    Prince William and Kate Are So Adorable on Their NBA Courtside Date in Boston

    Princess Catherine and Prince William took a break from their royal engagements to enjoy a low-key date in Boston. Yesterday evening, the two got courtside tickets to watch the Boston Celtics play against the Miami Heat at TD Garden arena. They were pictured laughing and cheering on Boston in laid-back looks.

    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Princess Catherine and Prince William took a break from their royal engagements to enjoy a low-key date in Boston.

    Yesterday evening, the two got courtside tickets to watch the Boston Celtics play against the Miami Heat at TD Garden arena. They were pictured laughing and cheering on Boston in laid-back looks.

    BRIAN SNYDER//Getty Images

    Kate wore a cobalt blue double-breasted tweed blazer by Chanel with black trim and glossy black buttons. She dressed down the elegant piece with a pair of dark gray jeans and black suede pumps by Gianvito Rossi. As per usual, she kept her makeup look natural and, on this occasion, left her hair down in silky waves. She accessorized with gold hoop earrings and a gold chain necklace.

    Samir Hussein - Pool//Getty Images

    William coordinated with Kate in a relaxed all-blue look. The Prince of Wales wore a casual suit in a dusty dark blue shade, paired with a light blue denim button-down. As for footwear, he traded his go-to dress shoes for brown suede shoes.

    Samir Hussein - Pool//Getty Images

    Throughout the game, the prince held onto a basketball he was gifted by the team. Photos also show both him and Kate watching the action on the court with excitement, whispering adorably to one another during the plays, and smiling and chatting with each other and with their seat neighbors.

    At one point, Kate was seen leaning in for a picture with Emilia Fazzalari, wife of Celtics owner Wyc Grousbeck, who was seated next to her.

    Samir Hussein - Pool//Getty Images

    The Wales's three-day visit to the United States comes ahead of their second annual Earthshot Prize Awards Ceremony, which will be held tomorrow (and airing on Sunday). In addition to partaking in fun Boston activities while they are on this side of the globe, the royals will learn about the environmental challenges the city faces and meet with those who are combating the effects of climate change in the area.
