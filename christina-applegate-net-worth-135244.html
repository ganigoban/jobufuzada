    Christina Applegate is an American actress born on November 25, 1971 in Los Angeles, CA. She is best known for her role as Kelly Bundy on the television comedy Married... with Children, as well as her role as Veronica Corningstone in the hit comedy Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy. Applegate has also starred in films such as Vacation, Hall Pass, The Sweetest Thing, and Just Visiting.

    About

    American Actress who played the saucy daughter, Kelly Bundy, on the television comedy Married... with Children. She also played news reporter Veronica Corningstone and love interest of Will Ferrell in the hit comedy Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy.
