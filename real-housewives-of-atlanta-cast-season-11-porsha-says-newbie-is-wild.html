    04 Sep 2024, 00:00

    A brand new season of The Real Housewives of Atlanta is here!

    Season 11 will air on Bravo TV on November 4th 8/7c (US) and in the UK it will be available to watch via streaming service Hayu on November 5th.

    The last season of RHOA was eventful, to say the least, cheating rumours, huge fallouts and questionable friendships came to a head in the season 10 reunion.

    So, who’s in The Real Housewives of Atlanta cast season 11? And is anyone new joining the show?

    Screen Shot: First Look at Real Housewives of Atlanta cast season 11 – Bravo TV

    Cynthia Bailey

    Last season there was a lot of drama around Cynthia and her kind-of boyfriend, Will Jones.

    That relationship never quite took off, but things are very different for Cynthia and new boyfriend, Mark Hill.

    She’s been talking about wedding bells!

    Prepare to see a completely different side to Nene this season.

    Things in her personal life take a turn for the worst but thankfully she has all her friends around her for support.
