    Oh, to be a fly on the wall Justin and Hailey Bieber’s closet.

    The couple—who always seem to be either coordinating baggy street fits or dressing for completely different events—just gave us two more looks to add to our winter mood boards. On Instagram, the “Peaches” singer shared photos of himself and his wife in cool cold-weather outfits, and the outerwear was the focus of both.

    The star of Hailey’s look was a glossy black leather trench coat, which she cinched around her waist with a built-in belt, and layered over some must-have staples: a gray sweater, baggy blue jeans, and cherry-red loafers. She accessorized with her go-to gold hoop earrings.

    Last year, the Rhode founder wore a similar trench paired with teeny hot shorts during Paris Fashion Week, as well as another look-alike trench, styled with sweatpants, while out in Los Angeles last summer. You can buy similar leather trench coats here.

    Justin’s outerwear of choice was an army-green puffer jacket with plenty of pockets and a tan shearling collar. The pop star wore the piece over a light blue V-neck button-up shirt, and paired it with oversize black trousers and chunky brown-and-white sneakers. He wrapped up the fit with a beaded silver necklace and a black baseball cap, which he wore backward. Similar jacket styles are available at Tom Ford, Mackage, and Woolrich.

    Justin didn’t caption his Instagram post, but Hailey left him a sweet note in the comments: “We’re cute.”
