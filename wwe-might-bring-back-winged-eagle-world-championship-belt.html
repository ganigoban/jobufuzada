    Last night’s Royal Rumble Premium Live Event turned out to be a huge success. Fans are still buzzing about what happened at the show. We got to witness a plethora of exciting moments and surprises. One of the biggest moments of the night was the conclusion that saw The Bloodline fall apart. Another big moment was the much-anticipated return of Cody Rhodes.

    Cody Rhodes entered the Royal Rumble match in the number 30 spot. He put on an impressive showing and lasted 15 minutes before he eliminated Intercontinental Champion Gunther to win the match.

    This win puts Cody directly in the path of Roman Reigns as he is headed to the main event of WrestleMania. However, it looks like Cody may be already counting his eggs before they hatch.

    The American Nightmare posted a photo of the old winged eagle WWE Championship belt on his Instagram indicating that he is considering bringing the title back again.

    It will be interesting to see how the storyline between Cody Rhodes and Roman Reigns unfolds in the months to come. Stay tuned to Ringside News as we bring you similar stories.
