    We’re partnering with the Grand Sierra Resort and Casino, northern Nevada’s premier resort destination, to bring world-class entertainment to the iconic Grand Theatre.

    Located in Reno, this historic theater is undergoing a $10 million renovation, with a redesign led by John Sergio Fisher & Associates. The revamped Grand Theatre will boast brand new artist facilities, including dressing rooms, green rooms and a stylish artist lounge. Venue capacity will be more than 3,000, featuring 2,700 luxury seats. Additional enhancements will include a stunning two-tiered entry with an all-new, enclosed lobby, state-of-the-art lighting and sound renovations. 

    Expected to reopen this summer, the Grand Theatre will host a special grand-opening performance. A full live entertainment lineup will be announced in the near future.

    For more information, visit www.grandsierraresort.com.
