    27 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Randy Orton made his return at Survivor Series back in November last year and in the months since then, firmly established himself as one of the top Superstars in WWE again. Apart from his wrestling stint, Orton is busy with other projects as he appeared in Eladio Carrion’s latest music video titled ‘RKO.’

    The Apex Predator is no stranger to popping up in the most random of places from time to time, especially during the current stage of his professional wrestling career. This includes even music videos.

    Popular rapper Eladio Carrion released his latest song titled ‘RKO’ which saw him portraying the role of a baseball player while singing. Towards the end of the video, Randy Orton makes his appearance by hitting an RKO on a cameraman.

    Randy Orton went into full fanboy made, as he made it clear he was ecstatic to be meeting Eladio Carrion. They shared a small hug before Orton stated that he would help Carrion with anything while he was in town. The video ended with Orton going back to get a sharpie and baseball cap for Carrion to autograph while also kicking the unconscious cameraman for good measure.

    This was certainly a great surprise for Randy Orton’s fans, as it was evident The Viper was having a blast throughout the video. We will have to wait and see whether Randy Orton will appear in more such music videos in the future.
