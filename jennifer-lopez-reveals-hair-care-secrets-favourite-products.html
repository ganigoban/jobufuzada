The good news is, they are all available to buy online. Some of them can even be snapped up on Amazon and they won’t break the bank

    Color wow Dream Coat anti-frizz hair treatment, £18.75 / $28, Amazon

    SHOP NOW

    If Jennifer is going to be strutting her stuff somewhere where humidity is a problem, she relies on this miracle spray to fight the frizz. Her hairstylist Chris Appleton prepped her hair with the Color Wow Dream Coat serum before she took to the stage at the Super Bowl in Miami. If you need any proof that it works, well – wind machines, dancing and numerous hair flips didn’t ruin her look, need we say more?

    Tangle teaser the ultimate hair brush, £13.93 / $11.44, Amazon

It’s a great brush for all hair types and it’s an absolute bargain!

    Dyson Supersonic Hair dryer, £299 / $399, Selfridges / Ulta Beauty

Well, it features cutting-edge tech that measures the air temperature 20 times per second to avoid and heat damage to the hair. After drying, to set her style and to get a really dynamic shape, JLo’s stylist puts clips at the roots and blasts the whole head with cool air.

    Color Wow Pop & Lock high gloss finish, £14.25 / $20, Lookfantastic / Color Wow Hair

    Jennifer’s stylist Chris has revealed the product he uses to finish styling on those natural curl days, once the hair is "about 98 per cent dry".
