    Calista Flockhart is an American actress born on November 11, 1964 in Freeport, IL. She is best known for her roles as Ally McBeal and Kitty Walker McCallister on Brothers & Sisters. For her performance in Ally McBeal, she won a Golden Globe Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award and a People's Choice Award. In 2015, she was cast in the CBS series Supergirl.
