    Emilia Clarke has arrived to Game of Thrones costars Kit Harington and Rose Leslie's wedding in Scotland in a romantic blush ensemble. The actress, known onscreen as Daenerys Targaryen (the latest love interest to Harington's Jon Snow, coincidentally enough) was all smiles as she made her entrance.

    Clarke layered a pink Mansur Gavriel coat over a leaf-printed pink dress, which she wore with slingback Dior pumps and a white handbag. She wore her Khaleesi-blonde hair down in a bob, with her brunette roots already starting to show.

    Getty Images

    We already got a hint that Clarke would attend the ceremony after she was spotted arriving in Scotland's Aberdeen airport yesterday. As expected, her appearance then was much more casualâ€”she wore white jeans, a bomber jacket, a scarf, sunglasses, and white flats for the trip.

    Following Clarke was another Game of Thrones star, Peter Dinklage, AKA Tyrion Lannister. The actor was all dressed up in a navy suit as he waved to fans. Like Emilia, he was also seen touching down in Scotland for the wedding yesterday.

    Getty Images

    Harington and Leslie's nuptials are taking place at Wardhill Castle, which the bride's family owns, in Aberdeenshire, Scotland. The wedding is a private event, but expect to see many more A-listers (especially GoT members) arriving to the venue.

    Harington specifically told his producer that he wanted the whole cast to attend the ceremony. "I was like, 'You need to factor in a Game of Thrones wedding by the way,' he recalled. "They [the cast] have all got to be there so the whole thing has got to shut down."
