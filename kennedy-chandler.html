    Kennedy Chandler is a well-known and rising basketball player in America who is having a successful career. He is rather young and has only recently entered the National Basketball Association. He was picked in the second round by the San Antonio Spurs, but he was moved to the Memphis Grizzlies, where he signed a four-year rookie contract.

    Who is Kennedy Chandler?

    Kennedy Chandler was born on September 16, 2002, in Memphis, Tennessee, USA. Chandler grew up in Memphis, where his parents raised him. From an early age, he was enthralled by basketball and enrolled at Briarcrest Christian School, where he began playing. Coming off the bench, he helped his team reach the semi-finals during his freshman season.

    He had a tremendous sophomore year, and at the end of his high school career, he earned multiple MVP trophies as well as DII-AA Tennessee Mr. Basketball. He later transferred to the University of Tennessee, where he played for the college team. He only played for his collegiate squad for one season. He signed with the NBA in 2022. Chandler hasn’t revealed anything else about his personal life.

    What is Kennedy Chandler’s age, height, and weight?

    Chandler is currently 21 years of age. He stands at a height of 6 feet 1 inch and weighs 77 kg

    What is Kennedy Chandler’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

    He holds American nationality and belongs to an African-American descent.

    What is Kennedy Chandler’s profession?

    Chandler began playing basketball in high school at Briarcrest Christian School, where he had some success. In his freshman year, he guided his team to the DII-AA semifinals. Later, in his sophomore year, he won the championship for his school team. He was also awarded the finals MVP. Chandler had an outstanding junior year, winning DII-AA Tennessee Mr. Basketball. In addition, he won the Nike Elite Youth Basketball League. Following his senior year, he was named to rosters for the McDonald’s All-American Game, the Jordan Brand Classic, and other events.

    He enrolled at the University of Tennessee in 2021 and played on the college team. He excelled in his freshman year in college, earning Second Team All-SEC and All-Freshman Team honors. He made his NBA debut in 2022 when he was picked in the second round by the San Antonio Spurs. However, he was dealt to the Memphis Grizzlies. The Grizzlies signed him to a four-year rookie contract. Chandler also earned a gold medal for his national team in the 2019 FIBA Under 19 World Cup.

    What teams did Kennedy Chandler play for?

    He played and currently playing for the Memphis Grizzlies

    What happened to Kennedy Chandler?

    Chandler was cut by the Grizzlies on a Saturday in April, with one game remaining in his rookie season, as a result of roster reshuffling to improve the team’s frontcourt depth following Steven Adams and Brandon Clarke’s injuries.
