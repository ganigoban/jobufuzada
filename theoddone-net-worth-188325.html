    Former professional video gamer with Team SoloMid, or TSM, he now games online under the TSM banner. He mostly streams on his tsm_theoddone Twitch channel, where he's gained over 850,000 followers, but occasionally uploads to his YouTube channel OddOneLOL.

    He always loved Gaming and his favorites are Starcraft, Warcraft 3, Fire Emblem, Civilization, Road Rash, and Hearthstone, before he got into League of Legends. He then went on to play that professionally with TSM during the first four seasons.
