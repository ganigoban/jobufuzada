On the show, she's come for Cardi B, announced new music, premiered new music, and done much more. She's always got a few surprises up her sleeve but today's edition of the show, which was recorded in London, was relatively non-eventful. The superstar may have let one major secret out of her bag by accident though, referring to her boyfriend Kenny Petty as her "husband" while speaking about him on-air.

    We all know just how close the couple is. Nicki Minaj seems to be extremely comfortable with him, telling fans that she would be down to have a baby with her longtime lover. Minaj and Petty have known each other for years but they only began dating last year. It appears as though their relationship may have already entered its next stage because many fans are curious after Nicki referred to Kenny as her husband. 

    "Every night when I get out of the shower, my husband takes this really good lotion that we just bought, and he massages my feet, and rubs them, and he rubs each toe individually, and the heel, and the ball of my foot, and he does it all," said the rapper on Queen Radio. While foot fetishists out there are probably really excited to know that Nicki's feet are being taken care of, this is probably a bit of information that many of us could have done without. Whether he's Nicki's husband or her boyfriend, the two seem to be happy together and that's all that matters.
