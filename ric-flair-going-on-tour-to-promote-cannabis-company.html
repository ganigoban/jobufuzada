    Ric Flair Going On Tour To Promote Cannabis Company

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Ric Flair’s love for the marijuana is nothing new. The Nature Boy revealed last week that he is going to be the face of new line of products for erectile dysfunction with a cannabis company. It looks like he would be to heading down to Michigan to promote the business.

    According to a report by MLive, Ric Flair would be touring various Michigan cannabis dispensaries on March 16th and 17th to celebrate the launch of his marijuana strain Ric Flair Drip in the state of Michigan. Customers would be able to meet The Nature Boy and take pictures with him.

    March 16th will see Flair tour dispensaries in Romeo, Mt. Clemens, and Madison Heights, MI. On March 17, Flair would be touring two Detroit area dispensaries. You can check out his schedule below to get even more info.

    March 16

    • Dispo Romeo, 100 Shafer Dr, Romeo from 11:30 a.m. to noon.
    • JARS Mount Clemens, 101 N Groesbeck Hwy, Mt. Clemens from 12:45 p.m. to 1:15 p.m.
    • PUFF Madison Heights, 2 Ajax Dr, Madison Heights from 3 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.

    March 17

    • Cloud Detroit, 16001 Mack Ave, Detroit from 2 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.
    • House of Dank, 3340 8 Mile Rd, Detroit from 4 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.

    Flair launched Ric Flair Drip last year through Mike Tyson’s cannabis company Tyson 2.0 and cannabis conglomerate Verano. Flair stands by cannabis, crediting it for helping him get off prescription sleep aids like Ambien and Xanax.

    Ric Flair is not the only person in the pro wrestling business to get involved in the legal cannabis industry. Last September, Rob Van Dam signed an exclusive licensing deal for his THC products after spending many years as the face of CBD brand . Former WWE superstar Sasha Banks on the other hand runs a CBD brand for which she won an award last October.
