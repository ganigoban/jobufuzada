    Mali+Lili Lola Beauty Bag Duo

    Leave it to Oprah to find a two-in-one toiletry bag that can be converted into a crossbody purse. Plus, it comes in this season’s hottest color (red!) and is on sale for $35. Mali+Lili’s Lola Beauty Bag Duo is made from vegan leather and includes a small zippered pouch and a larger drawstring bag with a removable crossbody strap. The best part is that the smaller pouch fits inside the bigger one, so you can easily keep your belongings organized. Choose from 10 colors, including trendy red, seasonal emerald green, or neutral black and beige. 

    Stripe and Stare Lounge Pant and Sweatshirt Set

    Oprah owns 12 sets of this Stripe and Stare loungewear, which is all the information we need to press “add to cart.” But, we’ll still fill you in on the deets. The two-piece outfit comes with a raglan-style crewneck sweatshirt and matching high-waisted joggers with pockets and a drawstring waist, both made from a soft tencel material. Oprah revealed to Oprah Daily that she wears the set as pajamas, workout clothes, and loungewear, making it a versatile addition to your wardrobe. 

    Lands Downunder All-Season Italian Herringbone Throw Blanket

    According to Oprah, this Lands Downunder throw blanket is “not too heavy or too light, not too big or too small, and not too expensive” — aka, the “Goldilocks option.” It’s made from a blend of cotton and acrylic that feels like a “slice a heaven,” per a reviewer, and it comes in more than 50 colors. The blanket will make the perfect cozy addition to your couch this winter, and it would also be a great gift for the homebodies in your life.  

    Ami Colé Desert Date Cream Multistick Lip and Cheek Tint

    As we’ve learned, Oprah is all about finding multi-use products, and that extends to the beauty space, too. This Ami Colé cream stick is a “lifesaver,” according to the mogul, since you can use it on both your cheeks and lips. The vegan and cruelty free formula comes in four shades, and it’s made with nourishing ingredients, like moisturizing baobab seed oil, antioxidant-rich hibiscus flower extract, and hydrating desert date oil. Throw this lip and cheek tint in your purse for quick and easy touch ups on-the-go.
